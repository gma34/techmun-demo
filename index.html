Empowering The World Since 1977

The Brooklyn Tech Model United Nations team strives to empower its members to discuss issues around the world, and to find ways to solve such issues. After even just a year of active involvement in our team, members leave with the knowledge of how they can impact the world and help solve its problems.  During meetings, members learn to debate their peers about serious issues and navigate the complex world of geopolitics through exploration of the various functions of the United Nations. During conferences, members apply the skills that they've learned and honed during our meetings to debate on problems and pass resolutions in Model United Nations committees.
